Evolution Odyssey

Evolution Odyssey is a board game where players take roles of different species within the ecosystem,competing for resourses and adapting to changing
environmental conditions. The game deepen your understanding on ecosystem evolution through playing intuitive evolution board game.
It is a fun an engaging way on how to learn biology concepts

Set up

Game board

Lay out the modular game board with the chosen ecosystem ;forest.

Species card

Each player selects a species card that outlines their species traits.

Event card

These represent triggering events such as ;drought, predators,flood,climate change,sudden increase in food.

Resource token

They contain food,water and shelter. They are acquired through interaction with the ecosystem.

Mutation tokens

These are modifications of the species traits. They are given as rewards to players who have survived the events and collected many resources.

Dice

To determine the number of steps to be moved by a player
