Introducing a grabber reacher tool

Creating a better everyday life for many people
with a lightweight picker tool

How it works

Grabber reacher tool is a lightweight,ease to use that's used to grap objects up. It's able to grab objects upto 31bs without damage
and also reduces labour intensity. The grabber activated with fingers has a trigger-activated mechanism that is used to pick up hard
and soft objects such as paper, plastic ,glass, cans, envelopes e.t.c

materials

Manilla paper

Masking tape

Scissors

Empty container

Box

procedures

Preparation of Materials

The box was cut in a straight small size using scissors and attached to the cellotape holder that was cut into two,to be used as a grabber. The arimis container is used to define the shape of our manila paper, and the manilla paper is cut and attached to the cellotape holder, using cellotape. Connect all the materials using cellotape.

Construction

The box was cut and shaped to form a handle suitable for supporting the middle part of the cellotape. The Armis container was cut into tubular form to support the box, and also, the manilla paper was added. The middle part of the cellotape roll was cut to make it easier to be able to lift items from one place to another. Cellotape was applied extensively to secure all parts together, ensuring the carrier could hold weight without falling apart.
